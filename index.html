STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES & VISION

Current Challenges

  • Manual Processes: Paper forms for tournaments cause data loss.
  • Uniform Issues: Stock issues and fitting problems with current Gi.
  • Hidden Churn: Students leaving due to financial hardship without asking for help.

Proposed Solutions

  • The Patch Model: Use the official Dragon Logo as a high-quality velcro patch. Students can buy WKF approved gear and attach the club identity easily.
  • Digital Voice: A discreet channel for financial or safeguarding support.
  • Centralised Feedback: Direct line to management.
