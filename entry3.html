Competitor Details

Sparring

Beginners: Non Contact  |  Intermediate: Non Contact to face, light contact to body  |  Advanced: Light contact to body & face  |  Black Belt: Semi contact to body & legs, light contact to head.
This is Continuous Sparring (not points).

Solo Kata

Beginners: Fuyu Kata Ichi or Nihunchin
Intermediate: Nihunchin, Saifa or Sanseru only
Advanced: Saifa, Sanseru or Bassai Dai only
Black Belt: Bassai Dai or any Black Belt Kata only

  • Within Sparring & Solo Kata divisions we will split into Male & Female divisions where necessary. From ages 8+ they will be separated in the Sparring section.
  • Kids Sparring ages 4–7 only need sparring mitts.
  • Sparring matches, everyone aged 8+ must have ALL required safety equipment: Gum Shield, Mitts & Shin/Instep Pads.

  • No Equipment = No Sparring = No Exceptions.
