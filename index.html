Strategic Objectives

1. The Power of Expression

Granting the formal right to be heard reduces internal tension and fosters ownership.

2. The Innovation Bridge

Cross-Dojo statistics and idea sharing create a permanent bridge beyond competition.

3. The Official Patch Model

Mandating a high-quality "Canton Official Velcro Patch" for club identity and revenue.

4. 360° Event Lifecycle

Mandatory pre/post-event digital feedback ensures every voice is heard.

5. AI-Assisted Efficiency

Digital categorisation routes issues instantly to the right department.

6. Digital Process Integration

Fully digital workflows for applications and reviews eliminate data loss.

7. Confidential "Digital Voice"

Encrypted channel for financial or safeguarding support without fear of judgement.

8. Centralised Management Line

A direct line to the Review Board ensures accountability for every suggestion.

SUCCESS METRIC

Target: Net Retention Rate following a 1-Month Pilot Phase.

Goal: Verify if "The Opportunity to Express" directly correlates to "Long-term Loyalty".

The Patch Strategy

PROBLEM

Sizing issues and stock costs with mandatory club Gis lead to dissatisfaction.

SOLUTION

The "Canton Official Velcro Patch" — a high-quality badge attached to WKF-approved gear.

OUTCOME

Higher student satisfaction (comfort) and maintained club identity.
